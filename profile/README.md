## 🏢 About Us

**MSC Consultoria** is a technology & AI consulting firm based in Rio de Janeiro, Brasil. We build intelligent systems for government, education, and digital products.

### 🎯 What We Build

| Area | Projects | Tech |
|------|----------|------|
| 🏛️ **Government** | DETRAN-RJ contract management, asset tracking, SEI integration | TypeScript, React, PostgreSQL |
| 🎓 **Education** | Arquimedes adaptive math platform, MSC Academy | React, AI Agents, LangChain |
| 🤖 **AI/ML** | Prometheus multi-agent framework, RAG pipelines, voice cloning | Python, LangChain, FAISS, HuggingFace |
| 🎮 **Gaming Analytics** | Dota 2 & LoL prediction systems | Python, XGBoost, scikit-learn |
| 📱 **Digital Products** | Festeja Kids event management, Recanto delivery | TypeScript, React, Stripe |

### 📊 Numbers

- **40+ repositories** across multiple product lines
- **21 HuggingFace Spaces** — AI agents, voice cloning, prediction models
- **21 HuggingFace Datasets** — gaming analytics, gov knowledge bases, training data
- **6 DETRAN-RJ systems** in production
